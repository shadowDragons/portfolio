Zhicheng Studio provides custom development for corporate websites, enterprise systems, and AI agents, with direct delivery, SEO-friendly structure, and no subcontracting.

---

Zhicheng Studio is an independent software studio that builds corporate websites, multilingual marketing sites, internal business systems, and practical AI-powered tools for companies that care about quality and long-term maintainability. The website at sphrag.com serves as both a service hub and a portfolio, showing the studio’s capabilities, delivery approach, and selected project directions.

Led by Junbin Zhong, a former technical lead from a large internet company, the studio focuses on direct communication and direct delivery without multi-layer subcontracting. This helps reduce information loss, keeps responsibility clear, and makes projects more predictable for clients who need serious execution rather than low-cost outsourcing.

A major part of the studio’s work is corporate website development, including company websites, foreign trade websites, multilingual sites, and service-focused marketing pages. These projects are approached as business assets, with attention to information architecture, content structure, SEO foundations, inquiry flow, and future extensibility instead of only surface-level design.

Zhicheng Studio also develops internal business systems such as ERP, OA, CRM, workflow platforms, approval systems, and other operational tools. These projects require a stronger understanding of business logic, permissions, data flow, and long-term iteration, and the site clearly reflects experience in building systems that support real company operations.

In addition, the studio works on practical AI applications and enterprise agents that connect large language models with real business workflows. This includes internal knowledge assistants, AI agents, automated content processes, and workflow tools that improve productivity and make better use of existing business systems and content.

Overall, sphrag.com presents Zhicheng Studio as a focused development partner for businesses that need well-structured websites, maintainable internal systems, and useful AI solutions delivered with direct accountability, realistic scope control, and long-term thinking.
